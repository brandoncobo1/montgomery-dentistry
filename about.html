Our Story

55 Years of Trusted Care

Montgomery Dentistry has been a cornerstone of dental health in Montgomery, Alabama since our doors first opened over five decades ago. Rooted in the community and dedicated to the families of the River Region, we have grown from a small practice into one of the area's most trusted and respected dental homes.

We offer comprehensive dental care under one roof — from routine cleanings and preventive care to cosmetic treatments, restorations, implants, and emergency dentistry. We accept most major dental insurance plans, offer flexible payment options, and always welcome dental emergencies. Whether you're a new patient or a long-time family member, you'll always feel at home here.
